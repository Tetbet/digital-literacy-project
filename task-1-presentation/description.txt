Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic
Tool Used: Canva (Free version)
Description:
This infographic was designed to help first-year batchmates understand the core concepts of navigating the digital world safely. It covers three main topics:
1. What Digital Literacy means and why it matters.
2. Essential safe internet practices (like 2FA and password management).
3. Professional email etiquette rules for college students.
The visual format makes it easy to read and share on college groups or forums.
