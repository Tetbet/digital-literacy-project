Stay Safe Online: Cybercrime Prevention Checklist for College Students

1. Financial Rule #1: NEVER enter your UPI PIN to receive money. A PIN is only used to deduct money from your account.
2. Financial Rule #2: Set strict daily transaction limits on your mobile banking and UPI apps to minimize potential losses.
3. Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on your email, social media, and banking apps.
4. Verify the identity of buyers/sellers on online marketplaces; do not trust ID cards sent over WhatsApp blindly.
5. Check URLs carefully before logging in; ensure the website starts with "https://".
6. Do not click on panic-inducing SMS links claiming your "bank account is blocked" or "electricity will be cut."
7. Never share OTPs (One Time Passwords) with anyone over a phone call, even if they claim to be bank officials.
8. REPORTING: If you are scammed, report it immediately to the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or call the Cyber Crime Helpline at 1930.
