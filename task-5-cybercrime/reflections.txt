Task5: Cybercrime
While researching UPI and online marketplace fraud, what surprised me most was the psychological manipulation scammers use. 
They artificially create a sense of urgency and exploit the victim's excitement of making a quick sale, causing even tech-savvy students to forget basic rules like "never use a PIN to receive money."
As a result of this research, one habit I will personally change is my approach to online transactions: I will no longer scan QR codes sent by strangers online, and I will strictly set low daily transaction limits on my UPI apps to minimize risk.
